STAR REACH

Expand your network across the galaxy

You begin as a small Outpost with enough energy to reach nearby stars. Double-click to connect. Hover for sensor data.

Upgrade your homeworld (select it, press U) to unlock new capabilities:

L2 Colony — 2-hop chains, relay branching
L3 Station — Unlimited hops, +10% efficiency
L4 Starbase — Build relay hubs (press H)
L5 Dyson Sphere — +20% efficiency, hub upgrades

Energy accumulates from your network. Spend it on connections and upgrades.

Discoveries grant bonus tech. Propulsion tech extends range.
